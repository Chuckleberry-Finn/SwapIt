title=Swap It
description=[b]Does the following:[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Equipped weapons will swap places with hotslotted weapons instead of being put away.
description=[*] Example: If you have Weapon A on your belt and Weapon B in hand- selecting weapon A will equip weapon A and put weapon B on your belt.
description=[*]Activating an empty hotslot will attach the equipped item if possible.
description=[*]Individual hotslots can be toggled via options menu.
description=[/list]
description=
description=
description=[h3][b]Required Mods:[/b][/h3]
description=[quote][b]In B41:[/b]
description= - EasyConfig offers options for customization.
description= - Both ErrorMag and Modding Alerts are optional.
description=[/quote]
description=[quote][b]In B42:[/b]
description=- EasyConfig is not usable/needed, the game now has native mod options.
description=- ErrorMag and Alerts are required.
description=[/quote]
